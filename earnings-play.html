The daily cycle runs down the middle: sleeve → earnings basket at 3:50 PM, earnings → sleeve at the next open. The side boxes are simply which sleeve you land in — SPY in uptrends, gold or cash in downtrends.
The bet: one night only. Buy stock just before the close, the company reports, sell into the next morning's move — up or down. Never longer.
Gate 1 · Flow. Smart-money call buying vs put buying must land in the green band. Too little = no signal. Too much = everyone's already in — that's the trap, not the trade.
Gate 2 · Expectations. A stock priced for perfection has nothing left to give on good news. The rules require the stock to be FALLING over the last two weeks — beaten-down with fresh bullish flow underneath.
Gate 3 · Track record. Last 4–8 reports: does this stock actually go up when it delivers? Some names sell off on every beat — those never qualify, no matter how good the flow looks.
Gate 4 · Calm tape. Fear is fine — fear spiking is not. When VIX jumps 20%+ above its own recent average, the night is skipped. The 35 nights this removed were losers in bull and bear years alike.
✓ flow + ✓ falling in + ✓ pays on beats + ✓ calm tape  →  LONG   ·   anything less → PASS
All four gates or nothing. There is no override — "but the flow is huge" at an all-time high is exactly the trade that loses.
