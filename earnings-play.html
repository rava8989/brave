How this works — in pictures
The bet: one night only. Buy stock just before the close, the company reports, sell into the next morning's move — up or down. Never longer.
Gate 1 · Flow. Smart-money call buying vs put buying must land in the green band. Too little = no signal. Too much = everyone's already in — that's the trap, not the trade.
Gate 2 · Expectations. A stock priced for perfection has nothing left to give on good news. Best setups look boring going in — with fresh bullish flow underneath.
Gate 3 · Track record. Last 4–8 reports: does this stock actually go up when it delivers? Some names sell off on every beat — those never qualify, no matter how good the flow looks.
✓ flow  +  ✓ not extended  +  ✓ pays on beats  →  LONG   ·   anything less → PASS
All three gates or nothing. There is no override — "but the flow is huge" at an all-time high is exactly the trade that loses.
