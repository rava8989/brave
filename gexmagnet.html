How this works — in pictures
1 · The map. Big option positions draw invisible lines on every stock. The white dot is the price.
2 · The trade. When price breaks above the flip, market-makers' own hedging tends to pull it toward the magnet. We buy the break, aim for the magnet.
3 · The exits. Every way out is decided before entry: target, stop, or timer. Zero feelings involved.
4 · The honesty rule. Every trade is scored against simply buying SPY the same days. If it can't beat doing nothing clever, it doesn't trade.
