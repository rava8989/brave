1/20 DTE Put Diagonal — Bullish Skewed
Entry — every trading day at 14:00 ET: sell a 1 DTE put 10 pts ITM, buy a 20 DTE put 20 pts lower than the short strike. E.g. SPX at 6300 → sell 1 DTE 6310, buy 21 DTE 6290.
Management — hold overnight. Next day roll both legs out 1 day for a credit. If a 1-day roll can't get a credit even with 10 pt strike adjustment, try a 2-day roll as close to price as possible.
Exit — typically hold until midday to capture theta decay from the now-0 DTE short, then roll. P/L comes from the diagonal price rising (short decays faster than long) — roll cost/credit is just bookkeeping.
