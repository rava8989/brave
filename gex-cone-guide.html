Intraday GEX cone — how to read it

The cone shows what total 0DTE dealer gamma usually does through the day (last 250 sessions), and where today sits against that history. One question, one glance: are the orange dots inside the blue band?

blue band = normal zone (middle 60% of days) blue line = typical day (median) orange dots = today (10:30 anchor + live readings)
1 · Orange inside blue — normal day
Dealer gamma is tracking its usual range. Dealers dampen moves, pins behave, M8BF trades on its normal rules.
2 · Orange far below blue — thin gamma → NO M8BF
Without dealer gamma, hedging flows amplify moves instead of pinning them. This is the regime the GEX gate skips: the fly is not posted and the day books as a no-trade. (M8BF only — Straddle, GXBF, BOBF and the Tail hedge run their own rules.)
3 · Orange above blue — fat gamma
More dealer gamma than 80% of recent sessions. The strongest pin conditions — historically the best butterfly days.

Why it matters — every M8BF-style fly at every 5-minute slot (no filters, ~35,900 trades, Sep 2024 – Jul 2026), grouped by where the day sat:
