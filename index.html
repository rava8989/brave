              Sigma 3 — SPX 0DTE Strategy Overview

              Sigma 3 consists of four SPX 0DTE strategies: M8 Butterfly (M8BF), Straddle, GXBF (GEX Butterfly), and BOBF.

              M8 Butterfly (M8BF)

              • Trades every week, every day Mon–Fri inside fixed 30-minute windows: Mon 11:00–11:30 · Tue 13:30–14:00 · Wed 12:00–12:30 · Thu 11:00–11:30 · Fri 13:00–13:30.
              • Enter the first qualifying center strike inside the window. One trade per day. Skip if none appears.
              • Banned center strikes (full): 10, 25, 35, 40, 65, 80 — bad on both T1 sides. Combo bans: T1=x00→x95 · T1=x20→x15 · T1=x55→x50 · T1=x65→x60 · T1=x85→x90 — bad on one side only.
              • Not traded on: EOM · EOM-1 · day before OPEX · VIX exp day (when VIX exp falls after OPEX) · all earnings days except AMZN and TSLA · day after any earnings (NVDA, MSFT, META, GOOG, AAPL) · NVDA earnings days. On EOM/EOM-1/day before OPEX/VIX exp day, M8BF is only allowed if previous day WR ≥ 90%. Use discretion on: first trading day of month · CPI day · Fed decision day.
              • No stop-loss — hold to expiration. Entry cost ≈ $14.

              Straddle

              • Used when overnight VIX drop is between 0.01 and 0.65.
              • Entry: 9:32 AM.
              • Not traded on OPEX days.
              • Not traded on Wednesdays — converts to M8BF (unless Fed, CPI, EOM, NM, or m8bfBanned day).
              • SPX gap ≥ 0.9% (up or down) → Straddle cancelled. No effect on M8BF or GXBF.
              • Open-to-open VIX drop > 1.4 → Straddle cancelled. Does not apply on EOM days. No effect on M8BF or GXBF.
              • Win rate = 0% prior day → next day is automatic Straddle regardless of VIX signal.
              • Win rate = 0% prior day on a Fed day → Straddle still blocked (Fed day takes priority).
              • EOM (last trading day of month) → automatic EOM Straddle @ 9:32 AM. BOBF remains in play. Max risk: $3,500.
              • NM (first trading day of month, non-Monday) → NM Straddle @ 9:32 AM. Max risk: $3,200.

              GXBF (GEX Butterfly)

              • Used when overnight VIX drop is > 0.65. Takes priority over open-to-open logic.
              • Entry: 9:36 AM, centered on major positive gamma.
              • Allowed on Fed days.
              • Automatic GXBF on OPEX+1 — overrides M8BF and Straddle signals that day.
              • Maximum VIX allowed: 25. Blocked if VIX ≥ 25.
              • OPEX+1 blocked if VIX gaps up ≥ 2% overnight (today's open vs yesterday's close).
              • CPI day takes full priority — OPEX+1 GXBF does not fire on CPI days.
              • Max risk: $1,400.

              BOBF

              • OptionOmega – BOBF base
              • Not traded on all 7 earnings days: MSFT, META, GOOGL, AMZN, AAPL, NVDA, TSLA.
              • Not traded on monthly VIX expiration days.
              • Not traded on OPEX.
              • Not traded on OPEX-1 (day before OPEX).
              • Not traded on EOM-2 and EOM-1.
              • Not traded on the first trading Monday of the month (NM Monday).
              • Maximum VIX allowed: 23. Blocked if VIX > 23.
              • Modified version traded on Fridays.
              • OptionOmega – Friday version

              CPI Rule

              • CPI day uses only Yesterday's Close − Today's Open (open-to-open is ignored).
              • VIX down → Long ATM Call @ 9:32 AM. Max entry premium: $13.
              • VIX up/flat on CPI day → M8BF (with all M8BF rules: banned strikes, time windows, combo bans). M8BF bans still apply (EOM, earnings, etc.).
              • CPI day overrides all other signals including OPEX+1 GXBF.

              NM & EOM Straddles

              • NM Straddle — first trading day of month, except Mondays. Max risk: $3,200.
              • EOM Straddle — last trading day of month. Max risk: $3,500.
              • Both use standard 9:32 AM Straddle entry.
              • Both override GXBF — if NM or EOM falls on a GXBF day, Straddle takes priority.
              • NM Monday follows the regular VIX calculator (no NM Straddle override).

              Win Rate Rules (Strongest Overrides)

              • WR = 0% prior day → forces Straddle. Trumps M8BF, GXBF, SPX gap block, o2o block — everything except CPI and Fed day.
              • WR ≥ 90% prior day → forces M8BF. Trumps Straddle, NM Straddle, SPX gap block, o2o block — everything except CPI.
              • Win rate = 0% on a Fed day → Fed day takes priority (Straddle not forced).
              • These are the strongest signals in the system. Gap and o2o rules only apply when neither WR=0% nor WR≥90% is active.

              VIX Expiration Rule

              • If VIX expiration falls after OPEX in the same month → M8BF blocked on VIX expiration day itself.
              • If VIX expiration falls on or before OPEX → this rule does not apply.

              Last 2 digits win rate — AM vs PM. Avoid low win rate strikes.

              Recent Changes

              • GXBF on OPEX+1 blocked when VIX gaps up ≥ 2% overnight — if today's VIX open is ≥ 2% above yesterday's VIX close, the automatic OPEX+1 GXBF signal is suppressed entirely.
              • SPX gap ≥ 0.9% cancels Straddle only — when the absolute SPX gap (today's open vs yesterday's close) is ≥ 0.9%, any Straddle or NM Straddle signal is cancelled. No effect on M8BF or GXBF. Gap and o2o have zero connection to butterfly.
              • o2o > 1.4 cancels Straddle only (except EOM) — when the open-to-open VIX drop exceeds 1.4, any Straddle or NM Straddle signal is cancelled. Does not apply on EOM days. No effect on M8BF or GXBF.
              • m8bfBanned days — M8BF is blocked on EOM, EOM-1, day before OPEX, and VIX exp day (when VIX exp falls after OPEX). Only a previous day WR ≥ 90% can override this ban.
              • WR=0% / WR≥90% now trump gap and o2o — these are the strongest overrides in the system. If previous day WR=0%, straddle is forced even if SPX gap or o2o would have blocked it. If WR≥90%, M8BF is forced regardless. Only CPI (and Fed for 0%) take priority.
              • VIX close corrected to 4:15 PM — all historical VIX/SPX close values now use 4:15 PM Schwab data instead of 3:59 PM ThetaData.
