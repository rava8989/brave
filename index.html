              Sigma 3 — SPX 0DTE Strategy Overview

              Sigma 3 consists of four SPX 0DTE strategies: M8 Butterfly (M8BF), Straddle, GXBF (GEX Butterfly), and BOBF.

              M8 Butterfly (M8BF)

              • Mon / Tue / Wed: requires M8BF signal — overnight VIX up, or open-to-open VIX > 1.4. Skip entirely if no signal that day.
              • Thu / Fri: trade every week — M8BF signal not required. On non-signal days it is optional but supported by data.
              • Enter the first qualifying strike that appears in the time window. One trade per day only. Skip the day entirely if no qualifying strike appears in the window.
              • Not traded on Fed days.
              • Not traded on MSFT and META earnings days.
              • Not traded on EOM-1 (day before last trading day of month).
              • Not traded post VIX expiration when VIX exp falls before OPEX in the same month.
              • GOOGL, AMZN, AAPL, NVDA earnings days — M8BF is allowed.
              • NM day (first trading day of month) and not a Monday → converts to NM Straddle @ 9:32 AM.
              • NM day falls on Monday → M8BF proceeds normally (no NM Straddle).
              • Post-OPEX Monday → M8BF proceeds but afternoon timing preferred.
              • Wednesday Straddle signal → converts to M8BF (unless Fed, CPI, EOM, or NM day).
              • Win rate > 90% prior day → next day's Straddle signal converts to M8BF.
              • No stop-loss — hold to expiration. Max risk: $1,400. Entry cost ≈ $14.

              Straddle

              • Used when overnight VIX drop is between 0.01 and 0.65.
              • Entry: 9:32 AM.
              • Not traded on OPEX days.
              • Not traded on Wednesdays — converts to M8BF (unless Fed, CPI, EOM, or NM day).
              • Win rate = 0% prior day → next day is automatic Straddle regardless of VIX signal.
              • Win rate = 0% prior day on a Fed day → Straddle still blocked (Fed day takes priority).
              • EOM (last trading day of month) → automatic EOM Straddle @ 9:32 AM. Max risk: $3,500.
              • NM (first trading day of month, non-Monday) → NM Straddle @ 9:32 AM. Max risk: $3,200.

              GXBF (GEX Butterfly)

              • Used when overnight VIX drop is > 0.65. Takes priority over open-to-open logic.
              • Entry: 9:36 AM, centered on major positive gamma.
              • Allowed on Fed days.
              • Automatic GXBF on OPEX+1 — overrides M8BF and Straddle signals that day.
              • Maximum VIX allowed: 25. Blocked if VIX ≥ 25.
              • CPI day takes full priority — OPEX+1 GXBF does not fire on CPI days.
              • Max risk: $1,400.

              BOBF

              • OptionOmega – BOBF base
              • Not traded on all 6 earnings days: MSFT, META, GOOGL, AMZN, AAPL, NVDA.
              • Not traded on monthly VIX expiration days.
              • Not traded on OPEX.
              • Not traded on OPEX-1 (day before OPEX).
              • Not traded on EOM-2 and EOM-1.
              • Not traded on the first trading Monday of the month (NM Monday).
              • Maximum VIX allowed: 23. Blocked if VIX > 23.
              • Modified version traded on Fridays.
              • OptionOmega – Friday version

              CPI Rule

              • CPI day uses only Yesterday's Close − Today's Open (open-to-open is ignored).
              • VIX down → Long ATM Call @ 9:32 AM. Max entry premium: $13.
              • VIX up on CPI day → No trade.
              • CPI day overrides all other signals including OPEX+1 GXBF.

              NM & EOM Straddles

              • NM Straddle — first trading day of month, except Mondays. Max risk: $3,200.
              • EOM Straddle — last trading day of month. Max risk: $3,500.
              • Both use standard 9:32 AM Straddle entry.

              Win Rate Rules

              • M8BF win rate > 90% prior day → next day's Straddle signal converts to M8BF.
              • M8BF win rate = 0% prior day → next day is automatic Straddle (BOBF can still be in play if not blocked).
              • Win rate = 0% on a Fed day → Straddle blocked, Fed day takes priority.

              Post-VIX Expiration Rule

              • If VIX expiration falls before OPEX in the same month → M8BF blocked on the first trading day after VIX expiration.
              • If VIX expiration falls on or after OPEX → this rule does not apply.
